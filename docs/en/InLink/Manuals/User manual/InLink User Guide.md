# **1\. InLink** **Introduction**

InLink is a cloud platform created by InHand Networks that is dedicated to SIM card management, aimed at providing users with convenient, fast, and secure SIM card management services. We offer the following services:

-   Real-time monitoring: Real-time monitoring of SIM card status and traffic consumption information.
-   Package management: Self-service package replacement service to save SIM card data plan costs.
-   Coverage of multiple operators: Reduce communication costs with multiple operators.

# 3\. Account and Role  

## 3.1 Account  

Currently, each organization can have only one account, which is the account created during registration. This account has the highest level of functionality and data access. If you need to add users, please contact us.  

## 3.2 Role  

The platform currently only offers the **System Administrator** role, which has all the platform's functional permissions and access to all data permissions within the organization.

## 4.1 Manage SIM Cards

Click **Actions** to manage the SIM card in batch. You can modify the SIM card status or data plan. You can also edit individual SIM card information in the SIM card list.  

  

![](images/img_75122813.png)  

### 4.1.1 **Activate**

1\. Select the SIM card you wish to activate. Only SIM cards in "**Inventory**" and "**Deactivated**" status can be activated, and they must have the data plan.

2\. Click "**Activate**" and confirm the action in the pop-up window.

3\. The SIM card list will display the activation status. If activation fails, there will be a notification message after the SIM card status.  

### **4.1.2 Change Data Plan**  

1.  If your SIM card doesn't have a data plan, you can assign a plan to it before activation.  
    
2.  If the data usage on your SIM card plan is insufficient, you can choose a different plan specification to optimize your costs.  
    

### 4.1.3 **Change SIM Info**  

Upload the file to Change the attributes for SIMs.  

1.Click on "**Change SIM Info**".

2.Download the SIM card information **template**, and complete it with the SIM card details.  

3.Upload the file and **submit** it. Currently, only changes to the SIM card's IMEI information are supported.  

  

![](images/img_8788576f.png)  

  

### **4.1.4 Deactivate**  

You can deactivate "Activated" SIM cards. Once a SIM card is deactivated, you cannot use its data traffic.

  

### 4.1.5 **Change Note**

This operation is used to modify the description information of a SIM card.  

  

## 4.2 Change Logs

Click **Change Logs**, you can view SIM card activation or deactivation operation records, so as to troubleshoot problems.

## 5.2 Make Payment  

The platform will generate bills for each billing period 1-2 days after the billing period ends. When you receive the bill, please make the payment promptly to avoid any issues such as SIM card deactivation or other anomalies.  

  

1.Select the unpaid bill and verify the accuracy of your billing information. If the information is correct, click "**Pay**". If there is an issue with your billing information, please contact us.  

  

![](images/img_deaa6f1d.png)  

  

2\. Choose your payment method and provide the necessary account details.  

  

![](images/img_1c56b87b.png)  

  

3\. If you need someone else to make the payment, you can generate an payment link to send to the respective person. Other users do not need to log in to the platform to make the payment.  

  

![](images/img_fb1636ff.png)  

  

4\. After a successful payment, the bill status will change to "Paid," and you can download the receipt.  

  

**| Note:**

①The platform supports credit card and bank account payments. If you need to make an offline payment, please contact us.  

②Bank account payments may take approximately 1-2 days to process, so please be patient.

# FAQ  

**1.What is the billing model of the platform?**

The platform operates on a postpaid billing model, which means you can use the data plan on your SIM cards first, and we will calculate your monthly data usage and associated charges at the end of the month. You only need to pay the monthly bill after it has been generated.  

  

**2.Why did the activation/deactivation fail?**

In most cases, it might be due to a request timeout. You can try retrying the operation. You can also check the specific reasons for the failure by going to the "Change Log" at the top of the SIM card list.  

  
  

**3.How to add another account within the organization？**

If you need to add users to your organization, please contact us.
