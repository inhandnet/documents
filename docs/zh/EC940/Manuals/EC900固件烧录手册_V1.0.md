## 烧录步骤

### 软件安装

- 解压DriverAssitant\_v5.12.zip
- 打开解压后的DriverInstall
- 点击驱动安装

![1736228930515-14074f9c-0982-40c2-b6b9-bbb3260c5a1c.png](./img/MRX4aMyFib2PusGJ/1736228930515-14074f9c-0982-40c2-b6b9-bbb3260c5a1c-481523.png)

- 提示安装成功后关闭软件

![1736230132674-18ff6e7c-ecbe-480e-8a63-85c110da262a.png](./img/MRX4aMyFib2PusGJ/1736230132674-18ff6e7c-ecbe-480e-8a63-85c110da262a-759755.png)

- 解压RKDevTool\_V3.18.zip
- 打开RKDevTool软件

### 连接设备

- 取下设备此处螺丝，打开设备侧面盖板

![1736230380624-a6ed51d2-2e95-4416-97b7-e417b9bf2f60.png](./img/MRX4aMyFib2PusGJ/1736230380624-a6ed51d2-2e95-4416-97b7-e417b9bf2f60-781112.png)

- 此处插上跳线帽

![1736230272935-f67f1d8b-2107-48ff-8522-bcbb70b82d0f.png](./img/MRX4aMyFib2PusGJ/1736230272935-f67f1d8b-2107-48ff-8522-bcbb70b82d0f-477044.png)

- 使用Type-C线连接到电脑

![1736230324239-74c8ecc5-e78d-4c0c-a655-7ed92f244f40.png](./img/MRX4aMyFib2PusGJ/1736230324239-74c8ecc5-e78d-4c0c-a655-7ed92f244f40-261082.png)

- 按住此按键，然后给设备通电，直到软件提示`Found One LOADER Device`后松开按键

![1736230347799-8a7295ce-e400-4ae5-a0ff-8386a78d6953.png](./img/MRX4aMyFib2PusGJ/1736230347799-8a7295ce-e400-4ae5-a0ff-8386a78d6953-547802.png)

![1736298350594-b35769ad-5eea-4dda-bb9c-2c6821b1f796.png](./img/MRX4aMyFib2PusGJ/1736298350594-b35769ad-5eea-4dda-bb9c-2c6821b1f796-299687.png)

### 烧录固件

- 点击`Upgrade Firware`
- 点击`Firmware`选择要烧录的固件文件

![1736231225407-f6bcea90-bd84-47bf-9319-6c960597e4f3.png](./img/MRX4aMyFib2PusGJ/1736231225407-f6bcea90-bd84-47bf-9319-6c960597e4f3-488084.png)

- 点击`Upgrade`

![1736231495799-4cfba622-8627-47f1-a504-31325dbf5192.png](./img/MRX4aMyFib2PusGJ/1736231495799-4cfba622-8627-47f1-a504-31325dbf5192-007668.png)

- 等待右侧提示`Download Firmware Success`则烧录完成

![1736231621068-1eb00690-cb5e-4cad-9525-6bd739149aaa.png](./img/MRX4aMyFib2PusGJ/1736231621068-1eb00690-cb5e-4cad-9525-6bd739149aaa-672238.png)

- 等待状态灯正常闪烁，表示系统启动成功
- 断开电源，跳线帽，Type-C线，盖上侧面盖板
